### Tracking Technologies and Cookies
We use Cookies and similar tracking technologies to keep an eye on activity on our Service and store certain info. The tracking technologies we use include beacons, tags, and scripts, which are all about collecting and tracking info and jazzing up our Service's analysis and improvement. The technologies we might use include:

- **Cookies or Browser Cookies**: A Cookie is a small file placed on your device. You can tell your browser to refuse all Cookies or to alert you when a Cookie is being sent. But hey, if you don't accept Cookies, you might not be able to use some parts of our Service. Unless you've adjusted your browser setting to refuse Cookies, our Service might just use 'em.
- **Web Beacons**: Certain sections of our Service and our emails might contain small electronic files called web beacons (also known as clear gifs, pixel tags, and single-pixel gifs). These snazzy little files let the Company (for example) count users who've visited those pages or opened an email, and they're also used for other related website stats (like recording the popularity of a certain section and verifying system and server integrity).

Cookies can be either "Persistent" or "Session" Cookies. Persistent Cookies chill on your personal computer or mobile device even when you go offline, while Session Cookies vanish the moment you close your web browser.

We use both Session and Persistent Cookies for the following purposes:

- **Necessary / Essential Cookies**
  - **Type**: Session Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are absolutely vital for hookin' you up with services available through the Website and enabling you to use some of its cool features. They help authenticate users and prevent fraudulent use of user accounts. Without these Cookies, the services you've asked for can't be provided, and we only use these Cookies to give you those services.

- **Cookies Policy / Notice Acceptance Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are used to check if users have gone ahead and accepted the use of Cookies on the Website.

- **Functionality Cookies**
  - **Type**: Persistent Cookies
  - **Administered by**: Us
  - **Purpose**: These Cookies are pretty handy, letting us remember the choices you make when you're using the Website, like recalling your login deets or language preferences. The whole point of these Cookies is to give you a more personalized experience and save you the hassle of re-entering your preferences every single time you use the Website.

For more deets about the Cookies we use and your choices regarding Cookies, hit up our Cookies Policy or the Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
